About Me

AI Engineer & Machine Learning Enthusiast

I'm Zoha Ashraf, a 4th-semester BS Computer Science student at FAST-NUCES (Chiniot-Faisalabad Campus), focused on Artificial Intelligence and Full-Stack Development. My journey into AI started with classical search and CSP algorithms in coursework and grew into building real applications powered by LLMs, like StackSense, built on the Anthropic Claude API — which placed 5th at the GDG Build with AI Hackathon 2026.

I work across the stack — Python, FastAPI and Oracle SQL on the backend, React on the front end — and I'm actively exploring NLP, generative AI and applied machine learning. My goal is to grow into a research-driven AI engineer who builds practical, intelligent products and shares what I learn through writing on Medium.
